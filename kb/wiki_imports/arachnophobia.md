## Summary

Arachnophobia is the fear of spiders and other arachnids, such as scorpions and ticks. Arachnophobia is a specific phobia and is one of the most common described anxiety disorders, despite the fact that most arachnids pose little threat to humans.
The word "arachnophobia" comes from the Greek words arachne and phobia.
