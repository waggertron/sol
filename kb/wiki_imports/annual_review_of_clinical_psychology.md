## Summary

The Annual Review of Clinical Psychology is a peer-reviewed academic journal that publishes an annual volume of review articles relevant to clinical psychology. It was established in 2005 and is published by Annual Reviews. As of 2024, the editor is Tyrone D. Cannon; previous editors include Thomas Widiger and founding editor Susan Nolen-Hoeksema.
As of 2023, Annual Review of Clinical Psychology is being published as open access, under the Subscribe to Open model.  As of 2025, Journal Citation Reports gave the journal an impact factor of 16.5, ranking it first out of 185 journals in the category "Psychology, Clinical (SSCI)" and fourth out of 93 journals in the category "Psychology (SCIE).
