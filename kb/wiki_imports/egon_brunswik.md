## Summary

Egon Brunswik Edler von Korompa (Austrian German: [ˈbrʊnsvɪk]; 18 March 1903 – 7 July 1955) was a psychologist who is known for his theory of probabilistic functionalism and his proposition that representative design is essential in psychological research.
