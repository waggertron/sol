## Key Contribution

HEXACO extends five-factor trait organization with a six-factor model:

- honesty-humility
- emotionality
- extraversion
- agreeableness
- conscientiousness
- openness to experience

The honesty-humility dimension is especially relevant for ethical and social
behavior, but it is also sensitive. It should not be casually inferred from weak
data.

## Model Implications

The platform should compare Big Five and HEXACO as candidate broad trait layers.
HEXACO may be useful where fairness, sincerity, modesty, or exploitiveness are
research-relevant, but product-facing labels should avoid moral judgment.

For generation, HEXACO facets may inform style and interaction only after strong
evidence and user confirmation.

## Limits and Cautions

Honesty-humility-related claims can become reputationally harmful. Store them as
high-sensitivity constructs unless the user explicitly opts into that domain.
