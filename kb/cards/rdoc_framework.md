# Source Card: RDoC Framework

## Citation

National Institute of Mental Health. Research Domain Criteria (RDoC).
https://www.nimh.nih.gov/research/research-funded-by-nimh/rdoc

Source registry id: `nimh_rdoc_framework`

## Domain

- clinical research framework
- dimensional mental health constructs
- affective and cognitive systems

## Key Contribution

RDoC is useful as a research orientation because it organizes mental processes
dimensionally across systems such as negative valence, positive valence,
cognition, social processes, and arousal/regulation.

## Model Implications

The project can borrow the habit of thinking dimensionally across systems
without claiming clinical measurement. For example, motivation, threat
sensitivity, social processing, and regulation may matter for generation, but
they should be treated as sensitive and uncertain.

## Limits and Cautions

RDoC is not a consumer personality model and not a diagnostic tool. It should be
used to inform research questions and caution, not product labels.
