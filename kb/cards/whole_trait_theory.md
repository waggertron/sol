## Key Contribution

Whole Trait Theory is useful because it treats traits as both descriptive
distributions of states and explanatory processes. A user's average tendency is
not the whole story; the range, variability, and contexts of trait expression are
also part of the model.

## Model Implications

The platform should represent:

- mean tendency
- variability
- context
- recency
- repeated state evidence
- explanatory hypotheses such as goals, appraisals, and motivations

This supports a profile that can say "often direct in work planning" while still
allowing "more exploratory and poetic in creative writing."

## Limits and Cautions

The explanatory side of Whole Trait Theory can be hard to measure from passive
data. User confirmation and direct elicitation are important.
