# Sol OCEAN Construct Blueprint v0

## Status

`experimental_design_review_only`

This blueprint supports candidate item design. It is not evidence that a Sol
instrument is reliable, valid, invariant, normed, or ready for product use.

## Reviewed Research Anchors

- `kb/cards/big_five_trait_structure.md`: OCEAN as a broad descriptive taxonomy,
  not a complete representation of a person.
- `kb/cards/construct_validity_psychometrics.md`: every construct requires an
  explicit nomological role, convergent/discriminant expectations, and ongoing
  validity evidence.
- `kb/cards/caps_person_situation.md`: behavior is context-dependent; broad
  scores must not erase if-then patterns.
- `kb/cards/whole_trait_theory.md`: trait standing summarizes a distribution of
  states and does not imply constant behavior.

## Blueprint Rules

- Measure self-reported tendencies, not identity facts.
- Avoid clinical symptoms, protected traits, morality, intelligence,
  competence, work eligibility, or risk judgments.
- Prefer ordinary-language behavior and preference statements.
- Treat the subconstructs below as candidate content coverage, not a confirmed
  factor structure.
- Each initial subconstruct receives one positive-keyed and one negative-keyed
  item to support design review; this balance is not itself validation.
- Product interpretation remains at `provisional_atom` / `review_only` even if
  a later pilot form is approved.

## Candidate Domain Blueprint

### Openness To Experience

Parent meaning: tendency toward curiosity, imagination, and engagement with
novel or aesthetically rich experience.

Candidate subconstructs:

1. `intellectual_curiosity`: interest in exploring questions and ideas without
   implying intelligence, education, or expertise.
2. `imaginative_exploration`: tendency to consider possibilities beyond the
   immediately given situation.
3. `aesthetic_engagement`: attention to and valuation of artistic, design,
   musical, or linguistic qualities.

Exclude:

- intelligence or cognitive ability
- political or religious ideology
- educational attainment
- novelty seeking that implies unsafe behavior

Expected related constructs: curiosity, imagination, aesthetic interest.

Main failure risks: abstract wording, education/culture loading, equating
conventional preferences with deficiency.

### Conscientiousness

Parent meaning: tendency toward organization, follow-through, and considered
action.

Candidate subconstructs:

1. `organization_structure`: creating workable order for multi-step activity.
2. `follow_through`: maintaining manageable commitments after initial interest.
3. `deliberation`: considering likely consequences before important choices.

Exclude:

- moral worth or virtue
- work ethic, employability, productivity ranking, or compliance
- perfectionism, compulsivity, or pathology

Expected related constructs: planning, persistence, orderliness.

Main failure risks: socioeconomic/context loading, social desirability,
confusing capacity constraints with stable tendency.

### Extraversion

Parent meaning: tendency toward social energy, outward expression, and active
participation.

Candidate subconstructs:

1. `social_energy`: typical energy response to enjoyable interaction,
   especially with familiar people.
2. `expressive_engagement`: visibly showing interest or reaction in group
   interaction.
3. `assertive_participation`: offering a view when participation is invited or
   useful.

Exclude:

- social skill, confidence, popularity, leadership quality, or competence
- anxiety diagnosis or impairment
- preference for large groups as inherently better

Expected related constructs: sociability, enthusiasm, assertiveness.

Main failure risks: cultural norms, accessibility, social anxiety, masking,
role expectations, and context-specific introversion/extraversion.

### Agreeableness

Parent meaning: tendency toward perspective-taking, cooperation, and respectful
engagement during interpersonal difference.

Candidate subconstructs:

1. `perspective_taking`: considering how a situation appears to another person.
2. `cooperative_orientation`: seeking workable outcomes that account for
   multiple parties' central concerns.
3. `respectful_conflict`: remaining direct without dismissing another person's
   concerns.

Exclude:

- morality, honesty, trustworthiness, gullibility, compliance, or submissiveness
- covert persuasion or conflict avoidance as a universal good

Expected related constructs: compassion, cooperation, politeness/tact.

Main failure risks: strong social desirability, cultural conflict norms,
mistaking directness for low concern, mistaking compliance for cooperation.

### Emotional Stability

Parent meaning: self-reported tendency toward recovery, emotional evenness, and
functioning under ordinary uncertainty. User-facing language should prefer
emotional stability or stress recovery over stigmatizing labels.

Candidate subconstructs:

1. `stress_recovery`: returning toward baseline after ordinary setbacks.
2. `emotional_evenness`: maintaining enough perspective during ordinary
   emotional reactions.
3. `uncertainty_tolerance`: continuing with activity when outcomes are not yet
   resolved.

Exclude:

- diagnosis, impairment, crisis, trauma history, self-harm, or risk status
- claims that emotional intensity is defective
- intelligence, competence, or reliability judgments

Expected related constructs: negative emotionality (inverse), emotional
reactivity, stress recovery.

Main failure risks: state contamination, current stressors, stigma, clinical
overlap, and treating contextually appropriate emotion as instability.

## Validity Questions For Later Review

For every proposed subconstruct:

- Does item content cover the intended meaning rather than a narrow scenario?
- What should it correlate with in a permissive benchmark instrument?
- What adjacent construct must it remain distinct from?
- Does wording function similarly across cultures, roles, accessibility needs,
  and life circumstances?
- How much response variance is social desirability or acquiescence?
- Does repeated administration show stable tendency plus meaningful state
  variation rather than noise?

No product or scientific claim should answer these questions before empirical
review.
